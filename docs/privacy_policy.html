CircuitWorkbench — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CircuitWorkbench ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our Android application CircuitWorkbench (the "App"), package name com.circuitworkbench.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the core features of the App. The following information is created and stored locally on your device only:

  • Circuit schematic files (.sch) you create and save
  • Simulation parameters and results
  • Component model libraries you build
  • App preferences and settings

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you make a purchase through the App, Google Play collects payment information on our behalf. We receive only:

  • Purchase confirmation and transaction token (to verify your Pro entitlement)
  • No credit card numbers, bank details, or billing addresses are received by us

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your location data
  • Your circuit designs or simulation results — these remain on your device
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Device identifiers

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we receive (purchase confirmation) is used solely to:

  • Verify your Pro entitlement
  • Restore your purchase if you reinstall the App or switch devices

We do not use any information for advertising, marketing, or sharing with third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All circuit files, models, and app data are stored locally on your Android device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data or circuit designs.

If you enable external storage in the App settings, your circuit files are saved to your device's Documents folder — this is still local storage on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the limited purchase verification data we handle, including using Google Play's secure billing infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Play Billing

The App uses Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases. When you make a purchase, you interact with Google's payment system. Google's collection and use of your payment information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5.2 No Other Third-Party SDKs

The App does not integrate any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social media SDKs, or other third-party tracking services. We do not display advertisements.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@circuitworkbench.com and we will delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data beyond purchase verification tokens, there is minimal personal data to access, correct, or delete. However, you may:

  • Delete all locally stored circuit files at any time through the App or your device's file manager
  • Clear all App data via Android Settings → Apps → CircuitWorkbench → Storage → Clear Storage
  • Contact us to inquire about any purchase records associated with your Google Play account

8. Data Retention

Circuit files and app data on your device are retained until you delete them. Purchase verification tokens are retained only as long as necessary to maintain your Pro entitlement. If you request deletion, we will remove any associated records.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the Effective Date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
