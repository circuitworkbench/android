CircuitWorkbench supports three theme modes, selectable in Settings under Theme.

LIGHT
The app uses a light background with dark text throughout. Schematic symbols render in dark colour on the light canvas.

DARK
The app uses a dark background with light text. Schematic symbols render in light colour on the dark canvas. Dark mode is easier on the eyes in low-light environments and reduces battery consumption on OLED screens.

SYSTEM DEFAULT
The app follows the system-wide dark/light mode setting on your device. If your device switches between light and dark automatically (e.g. based on time of day or ambient light), CircuitWorkbench switches with it.

CHANGING THE THEME
Go to Settings and tap Theme. Select Light, Dark, or System Default from the list. The change takes effect immediately — the entire app redraws in the new theme without restarting.

The theme applies to all screens in the app including the schematic editor, simulation results, model library, and all settings screens.
